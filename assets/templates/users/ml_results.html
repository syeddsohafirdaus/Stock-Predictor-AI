Understanding the Metrics

📉 Mean Squared Error (MSE)

MSE measures the average of the squares of the errors. A lower MSE indicates that the model predictions are closer to the real stock prices.

📈 Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE)

RMSE represents the square root of the MSE. It gives the error in the original units (USD), indicating on average how many dollars the forecast deviates from the actual closing prices.

🚀 Platform Suggestion

For volatile tech assets like Amazon (AMZN), RMSE values are expected to fluctuate. We recommend retraining the model regularly as fresh daily transaction data becomes available.
